Protection Site
This site provides current crime information in Kumasi Metropolitan Assembly. Materials on this page are for the purpose of crime control. It is for policing and civilians. Circles are high risk zones! Follow thses prectices to prevent yourself from being the victim to crimes in the KMA
Take it into mind that everyone is a suspect (Dep. CID secretary, Adum). Market places (Adum, Assafo, Kejetia) are full of thiefs, snachers and frauds so be causion with your belongings all the time. For busy areas (Kejetia, Adum, Asafo, Bantama, Roman Hill, Race course), it is advisable to be in the crowd especially on the roads.
Those wwith kids must hold them by the hand very tight. It is more advisable to visit shops to buy some goods like electrical gadgets to prevent being defrauded.
Be discipline and do not talk to you do not know. Trust no one. Silenctly report any unpleasant act to the cops around.
Do not engage in fights especial inn the market and lorry stations. Do not leave kids for others you do not know. Stay safe!


Control Measures
Circles are high risk zone!

Some of these measures are already in place, some are yet to be done and others are sugestions.

Areas within the circle must be highly combatted.

In total, theft, fraud and murder are common in the metropolis.

Surveillance and deployment of many cops could help reduce the thefts and asults.
